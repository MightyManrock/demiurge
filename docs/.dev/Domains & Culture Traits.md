* culture:ancestor_worship

* culture:luminary_worship (can be linked to specific Luminaries or the pantheon as a whole)
* culture:demiurge_worship

* culture:maltheism (indicates broad hostility toward Luminaries and the Demiurge)
* culture:void_worship (indicates reverence for the Underreal and related entities)
