# Light

**Thematic arc:** Natural illumination and the guiding signal → Revelation and the mirror of truth → Blinding radiance and the shared procession → The Undimmed.

## Tier 1

### The First Rays

**Tooltip Blurb:** *Darkness does not end dramatically. It simply stops being true.*

**Description:** Light is natural rhythm, the dawn that arrives regardless of whether anyone was waiting for it, the cycle that asks nothing.

**Mechanics:** `light:0.35, water:0.1, decay:-0.1, animism:0.2, idealism:0.15`

### The Beacon

**Tooltip Blurb**: *A beacon is not for the one who lit it. That is the whole point.*

**Description:** Light is guidance: the lighthouse, the signal fire, the flame made specifically to be seen by someone who needs it—illumination in service of others.

**Mechanics:** `light:0.3, community:0.1, silence:-0.1, cooperation:0.15, honesty:0.15`

## Tier 2

### The Opened Eye

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The moment of understanding does not feel like gaining something. It feels like losing the option of not knowing.*

**Description:** Light enables comprehension, through revelation that creates the condition of understanding where ignorance was before. But there is a cost to seeing clearly.

**Mechanics:** `light:0.55, sacrifice:0.15, void:-0.05, erudition:0.2, honesty:0.15, idealism:0.15`

### The Mirror

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The mirror is the most honest thing in the room. This is why people avoid it.*

**Description:** Light reflects the true image returned unchanged, the self made visible to itself. It can be the instrument of self-knowledge rather than knowledge of the world.

**Mechanics:** `light:0.5, memory:0.12, change:-0.04, honesty:0.2, humility:0.2, sincerity:0.15`

## Tier 3 — Left (requires The Opened Eye)

### The Blinding Radiance

**Tooltip Blurb:** *There is a point past which revelation is no longer useful. This has never stopped revelation.*

**Description:** Light may be weaponized as illumination so total it destroys rather than reveals. Becoming truth at an intensity that cannot be survived, the god shows itself fully to a mortal who asked.

**Mechanics:** `light:0.8, conflict:0.45, void:-0.02, honesty:0.4, tenacity:0.2, idealism:0.2, competition:0.1`

## Tier 3 — Right (requires The Mirror)

### The Lantern Procession

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The light that is passed from hand to hand arrives somewhere that no single lamp could reach.*

**Description:** Light carried and shared, a ritual of passing illumination through a community, is the procession in which each person is briefly the source—and everyone gets a turn.

**Mechanics:** `light:0.75, community:0.4, secrecy:-0.02, cooperation:0.45, luminary_worship:0.2, humility:0.2, patience:0.1`

## Tier 4 (requires all previous)

### The Undimmed

**Tooltip Blurb:** *Light is the condition of complete illumination, the state in which nothing remains unseen and nothing is hidden.*

Description: The divine concept of Light itself.

**Mechanics:** `light:1.0`
