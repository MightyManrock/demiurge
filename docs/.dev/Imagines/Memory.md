# Memory

**Thematic arc:** Personal recollection and oral tradition → The archive and the wound that won't close → The civilization that lives in its past or the grief that becomes identity → The Eternal Record.

## Tier 1

## The Elder's Tale

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The oldest stories were never written down, which is why they survive.*

**Description:** What lives in the mouth and passes to another mouth carries something the inscription loses: the weight of the person who chose to remember it. The story told by a living voice is not the same as the story carved in stone.

**Mechanics:** `memory:0.35, fire:0.1, mastery:-0.1, ancestor_worship:0.2, sincerity:0.15`

### The Old Scar

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The body keeps its own records, written in the language of damage and healing.*

**Description:** What the mind prefers to forget, the scar refuses to. Memory that lives in the body does not require consent.

**Mechanics:** `memory:0.3, conflict:0.1, growth:-0.1, competition:0.1, tenacity:0.2, folk_wisdom:0.1`

## Tier 2

### The Stone Tablet

**Tooltip Blurb:** *What is carved in stone resists the forgetting that takes everything else.*

**Description:** The record that outlasts the civilization that made it is the most honest thing that civilization ever produced. It does not know what was forgotten alongside it.

**Mechanics:** `memory:0.55, mastery:0.15, fire:-0.05, sedentism:0.15, erudition:0.2, patience:0.15`

### The Restless Ghost

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The ghost does not haunt because it wishes to; it haunts because it does not know how to stop.*

**Description:** What refuses to leave the present was not finished with it, or the present was not finished with what refuses to leave. The distinction rarely matters to either party.

**Mechanics:** `memory:0.5, void:0.12, growth:-0.04, ancestor_worship:0.15, sincerity:0.15, patience:0.1`

## Tier 3 — Left (requires *The Stone Tablet*)

## The Hall of Ancestors

**Tooltip Blurb:** *As you walk through where the dead sleep, remember that the dead once walked where you do elsewhere.*

**Description:** A civilization that consults its dead before making decisions has decided the accumulated weight of those who came before outweighs the freedom of those here now. It is sometimes correct to heed the dead, and sometimes the dead are simply preventing the living from making the necessary mistakes.

**Mechanics:** `memory:0.8, order:0.45, conflict:-0.02, ancestor_worship:0.5, hierarchy:0.2, patience:0.2, humility:0.1`

## Tier 3 — Right (requires *The Restless Ghost*)

### The Wound That Festers

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The wound that was not allowed to heal becomes the wound that defines everything.*

**Description:** What a people have suffered becomes, in time, what they believe they are. Whether this is cause for mourning or solidarity depends entirely on who is asking.

**Mechanics:** `memory:0.75, water:0.4, growth:-0.02, xenophobia:0.15, tenacity:0.3, sincerity:0.2, idealism:0.1`

## Tier 4 *(requires all previous)*

### The Eternal Record

**Tooltip Blurb:** *Memory is the accumulated record of what persists and endures, true or not.*

**Description:** The divine concept of Memory itself.

**Mechanics:** `memory:1.0`
