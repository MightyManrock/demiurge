# Fire

**Thematic arc:** Sacred flame and sacrificial burning → The disciplined forge and the communal hearth → Devoted passion and wandering desire → The Eternal.

## Tier 1

### The Holy Flame

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The flame on the altar is not warming anything. It is a message, whether the gods receive it or not.*

**Description:** The altar flame, the vestal fire, the burning that reaches upward—sacred fire is the most ancient form of divine communication.

**Mechanics:** `fire:0.35, light:0.1, memory:-0.1, animism:0.2, idealism:0.15`

### The Billowing Pyre

**Tooltip Blurb:** *What is given to the fire is given completely. This is what makes it a gift.*

**Description:** The sacrificial pyre is the great burning that gives everything and asks for the same. It is destruction as the highest form of devotion.

**Mechanics:** `fire:0.3, sacrifice:0.1, growth:-0.1, animism:0.15, tenacity:0.1`

## Tier 2

### The Heat of the Forge

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The sword does not emerge from the fire unchanged, and neither does the smith.*

**Description**: The kiln, the furnace, the productive use of heat—as a disciplined craft, fire makes things rather than consuming them.

**Mechanics:** `fire:0.55, mastery:0.15, change:-0.04, sedentism:0.15, tenacity:0.2, pragmatism:0.15`

### The Warming Coals

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The fire at the center of the room is the room. Everything else is just walls.*

**Description:** Domestic warmth, the hearth around which people gather, is the flame that defines home by defining where home is.

**Mechanics:** `fire:0.5, community:0.15, silence:-0.04, sedentism:0.2, patience:0.15, moderation:0.1`

## Tier 3 — Left (requires The Heat of the Forge)

### The Hearts Entwined

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The fire that commits does not burn less brightly. It burns more precisely.*

**Description:** Passionate, devoted love is intensity through fidelity, the fire that chooses one thing and gives itself to that thing entirely.

**Mechanics:** `fire:0.8, truth:0.45, change:-0.02, monogamy:0.5, sincerity:0.2, patience:0.15, idealism:0.1`

## Tier 3 — Right (requires The Warming Hearth)

### The Errant Lover

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The fire that spreads is not less fire. It is simply honest about its nature.*

**Description:** This is fire as abundance, love that does not believe limitation is the same as devotion. Passion moves freely, finding no contradiction in warmth given to many.

**Mechanics:** `fire:0.75, growth:0.4, order:-0.02, polygamy:0.45, adaptability:0.2, wit:0.2, indulgence:0.1`

## Tier 4 (requires all previous)

### The Eternal Flame

**Tooltip Blurb:** *Fire is the heat of raw passion that precedes and outlasts every individual burning.*

**Description:** The divine concept of Fire itself.

**Mechanics:** `fire:1.0`
