# Order

## Tier 1

### The Clenched Gauntlet

**Tooltip Blurb:** *Authority burns away the contradictions of the past.*

**Description:** Imposed order demands official history over lived memory; the fist that enforces rewrites what came before it.

**Mechanics:** `order:0.35, fire:0.08, memory:-0.1, hierarchy:0.2, tenacity:0.15, honesty:0.1`

### The Warden's Mark

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The price of a stable world is the closing of doors.*

**Description:** Maintaining order costs something—of freedom, of exception, of the individual. But the warden's jurisdiction also resists what disrupts its categories; the new finding that overturns established truth is unwelcome here.

**Mechanics:** `order:0.3, sacrifice:0.1, mastery:-0.1, sedentism:0.15, moderation:0.15`

## Tier 2

### The Iron Compact

**Tooltip Blurb:** *What is remembered cannot be undone.*

**Description:** Binding agreements crystallize into memory, the oath that cannot be forgotten, the treaty that becomes its own keeper. But compacts resist organic expansion; the signed border that prevents the natural spread.

**Mechanics:** `order:0.55, memory:0.15, growth:-0.05, diplomacy:0.15, honesty:0.2, patience:0.1`

### The Measured Path

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The shortcut is always longer.*

**Description:** Patient, ordered process is the only route to true mastery, discipline that transforms over time into expertise. But the ordered path does not demand sacrifice; it demands patience. Sacrifice is for those without systems.

**Mechanics:** `order:0.5, mastery:0.12, sacrifice:-0.04, cooperation:0.2, moderation:0.2, pragmatism:0.15`

## Tier 3 — Left *(requires The Iron Compact)*

### The Eternal Throne

**Tooltip Blurb:** *Power is proven by what it outlasts.*

**Description:** The throne endures while everything around it decays, but decay is also what validates the throne's permanence. The institution that outlasts civilizations proves its own necessity by feeding on their ruin. The still water resists flow.

**Mechanics:** `order:0.8, decay:0.45, water:-0.02, hierarchy:0.5, luminary_worship:0.15, ambition:0.2, humility:0.1`

## Tier 3 — Right *(requires The Measured Path)*

### The Silent Architecture

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The cage is most effective when the bars are invisible.*

**Description:** Structures define by what they exclude: walls create emptiness, laws create silence, categories create void where the uncategorized falls. The architecture of control does not serve community; it supersedes it.

**Mechanics:** `order:0.75, void:0.4, community:-0.02, hierarchy:0.45, isolationism:0.2, pragmatism:0.2, patience:0.15`
