# Secrecy

**Thematic arc:** The kept confidence and the locked room → The double life and the formally protected secret → The conspiracy that binds and the tradition that preserves → The Impenetrable Veil.

## Tier 1

### The Kept Confidence

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The secret held for someone else is not yours to do anything with.*

**Description**: What is both the simplest and the most difficult thing about bearing the secrets of others is that what is given in trust must be given back in kind, or the next secret will not be given at all.

**Mechanics:** `secrecy:0.35, sacrifice:0.1, conflict:-0.1, culture:cooperation:0.15, culture:sincerity:0.15`

### The Locked Room

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The room no one else can enter is the room that belongs entirely to you.*

**Description:** Your own is the only space in which you are not shaped by being observed. What happens there is no one's business but yours, which is a rarer condition than it sounds.

**Mechanics:** `secrecy:0.3, memory:0.1, order:-0.1, culture:isolationism:0.15, culture:patience:0.2`

## Tier 2

### The Double Life

**Tooltip Blurb:** *To live two lives simultaneously is to become expert in the gap between them.*

**Description:** The slight adjustment of word and posture and emphasis that is the difference between what others see and what is true is exhausting but, for some, the only way they know how to be safe.

**Mechanics:** `secrecy:0.55, mastery:0.15, growth:-0.05, culture:competition:0.1, culture:adaptability:0.2, culture:wit:0.2, culture:pragmatism:0.1`

### The Sealed Vault

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The formal protection of a secret is an acknowledgment that it has value.*

**Description:** What is locked away is worth the cost of locking it. The vault makes explicit what the locked room leaves implicit: some things are not for everyone.

**Mechanics:** `secrecy:0.5, decay:0.12, mastery:-0.04, culture:hierarchy:0.15, culture:patience:0.2, culture:pragmatism:0.15`

## Tier 3 — Left (requires *The Double Life*)

### The Inner Circle

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The group that knows becomes, through the knowing, something more cohesive than groups formed by geography or blood.*

**Description:** Shared secrets are among the strongest bonds there are, which is why they are so frequently exploited. The inner circle is a community built on exclusion, which is a different thing from a community built on belonging—though from inside, it is hard to tell.

*(Special Rule candidate: Community expression generated by this Imago is not subject to standard suppression from Secrecy domain expression. The conspiracy creates community through secrecy rather than despite it.)*

**Mechanics:** `secrecy:0.8, community:0.45, truth:-0.02, culture:hierarchy:0.5, culture:ambition:0.2, culture:pragmatism:0.15, culture:wit:0.1`

## Tier 3 — Right (requires *The Sealed Vault*)

### The Ancient Mystery

**Tooltip Blurb:** *What has been deliberately hidden for long enough begins to feel like it was always meant to be hidden.*

**Description:** The tradition so old that its origins are lost, the knowledge only initiates understand—whether the secret is still worth keeping rarely gets asked, because asking would mean someone already knew the answer.

**Mechanics:** `secrecy:0.75, memory:0.4, light:-0.02, culture:ancestor_worship:0.2, culture:erudition:0.3, culture:patience:0.2, culture:humility:0.1`

## Tier 4 *(requires all previous)*

### The Impenetrable Veil

**Tooltip Blurb:** *Secrecy is the active maintenance of concealment, the work of keeping what is hidden from becoming known.*

**Description:** The divine concept of Secrecy itself.

**Mechanics:** `secrecy:1.0`
