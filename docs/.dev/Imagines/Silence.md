# Silence
## Tier 1

### The Masked Face

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The revealed god is already diminished.*

**Description:** Silence is the void between sounds, absence as its own kind of presence. But the masked one does not sacrifice themselves; revelation is the deepest sacrifice, and they refuse it entirely.

**Mechanics:** `silence:0.35, void:0.1, sacrifice:-0.1, nontheism:0.15, patience:0.2`

### The Still Pool

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The surface that moves reflects nothing.*

**Description:** Stillness holds reflections; memory lives in silence, the pool that records without disturbing. But stillness does not grow; the still pool resists the seasonal flood, the living current.

**Mechanics:** `silence:0.3, memory:0.08, growth:-0.1, isolationism:0.15, humility:0.2`

## Tier 2

### The Empty Throne

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The throne that is never sat upon is never challenged.*

**Description:** Power preserved in absence also slowly rots—the paradox of ruling by not ruling, the empty throne that decays precisely because no one maintains it. And yet the decay validates the absence; power that rots proves it was real.

**Mechanics:** `silence:0.55, decay:0.15, community:-0.05, hierarchy:0.15, pragmatism:0.2, patience:0.15`

### The Withheld Breath

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The word kept is the word that wins.*

**Description:** The fire never lit—potential energy, a burning that doesn't consume. The withheld word is a flame kept in the throat. But restraint resists the impulse to investigate, to reveal, to find out; the withheld breath does not ask questions.

**Mechanics:** `silence:0.5, fire:0.12, mastery:-0.04, cooperation:0.15, patience:0.2, wit:0.15`

## Tier 3 — Left *(requires The Empty Throne)*

### The Hidden Hand

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The string is only useful if the puppet doesn't know it's there.*

**Description:** The hidden hand *requires* community to operate; it works through social networks, through whispers and debts and relationships. Invisible governance runs on connection while denying connection. But it cannot survive full revelation; truth is its only vulnerability.

**Mechanics:** `silence:0.8, community:0.45, truth:-0.02, hierarchy:0.4, nontheism:0.2, pragmatism:0.25, wit:0.15`

## Tier 3 — Right *(requires The Withheld Breath)*

### The Patient Watcher

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The god who knows everything and says nothing is the most terrible god of all.*

**Description:** The patient observation that eventually reveals truth without acting on it—the watcher accumulates truth through silence. But the patient watcher gives nothing of themselves; they do not sacrifice their detachment for the comfort of intervention.

**Mechanics:** `silence:0.75, truth:0.4, sacrifice:-0.02, luminary_worship:0.45, nontheism:0.2, patience:0.3, humility:0.15`

### The Absent God
