# Void

**Thematic arc:** Pure absence and the liminal threshold → The abyss that draws and the hollow within → The darkness that consumes and the emptiness that creates → The Null.

## Tier 1

### The Empty Quarter

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The emptiness is not a lack of something. The emptiness is the thing.*

**Description:** Most things that are empty are waiting to be filled, yet some are not waiting for anything. To sit with emptiness that has no use and no intention is the beginning of a rare form of patience.

**Mechanics:** `void:0.35, truth:0.1, conflict:-0.1, nontheism:0.15, patience:0.2`

### The Nothing in Between

**Tooltip Blurb:** *There is a moment between one thing and the next that belongs to neither.*

**Description:** Every transition passes through a moment that belongs to neither what came before nor what comes after, a gap most minds hurry across without looking down. The cultures that learn to treat the threshold as a place rather than a passage find in it something more solid ground cannot offer.

**Mechanics:** `void:0.3, change:0.1, order:-0.1, animism:0.15, adaptability:0.2`

## Tier 2

### The Boundless Abyss

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The abyss does not look back at you, for it cares not for anything.*

**Description:** The abyss does not require anything from those who face it, which is what makes it so difficult to face. What returns from the encounter is not the abyss's gift; it is whatever was in the person all along, reflected from a very great depth.

**Mechanics:** `void:0.55, sacrifice:0.15, mastery:-0.05, void_worship:0.2, humility:0.2, patience:0.15`

### The Hollow Space

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The space inside the bell is not separate from the bell but its most crucial part.*

**Description:** Function depends on absence as much as presence: the bowl that cannot hold, the bell that cannot ring. Emptiness is not the failure of the form but the condition of its purpose.

**Mechanics:** `void:0.5, water:0.12, truth:-0.04, nontheism:0.15, humility:0.2, adaptability:0.15`

## Tier 3 — Left (requires The Abyss)

### The Devouring Dark

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The dark does not hate what it consumes, but that too is reason to fear it.*

**Description:** The dark that consumes does not hate what it takes, and this is the most terrible thing about it. Hatred would at least imply a relationship, something to appeal to, and yet it is not to be found.

**Mechanics:** `void:0.8, conflict:0.45, light:-0.02, void_worship:0.5, maltheism:0.1, tenacity:0.2, pragmatism:0.15`

## Tier 3 — Right (requires The Hollow)

### The Vacant Womb

**Tooltip Blurb:** *Nothing is not the absence of possibility but what possibility is made from.*

**Description:** The womb before conception holds nothing and is therefore capable of holding everything; the canvas before the first mark contains all possible paintings. An absence is a readiness.

**Mechanics:** `void:0.75, mastery:0.4, conflict:-0.02, nontheism:0.2, idealism:0.3, patience:0.2, humility:0.1`

## Tier 4 (requires all previous)

### The Null

**Tooltip Blurb:** *Void is the fundamental condition of absence from which all presence is distinguished.*

**Description**: The divine concept of Void itself.

**Mechanics:** `void:1.0`
