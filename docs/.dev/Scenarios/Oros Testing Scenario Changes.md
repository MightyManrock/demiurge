### Factions

#### Asha Dunewalker Clan

Wide-faring martial clan led by the warchief Asha Keln. Pops in the Dunes of Tor and Taem's Oasis belong to this faction; they also consider the Salt Flats their territory, and they often make incursions into both the Plains of Kir'an and the Asvelim Savannah.

#### The Hiparunites

Highland and canyon-dwelling folk led by a council of elders unofficially headed by Kael Osh; they mainly reside in Hiparun's Rift but also guard the Ulum Highlands from outsiders. Like Asha Dunewalker Clan, they occasionally raid the Plains of Kir'an and the Asvelim Savannah.

#### The Stonecallers

Religious tradition of veneration for the Ancestor Stones, led by the prophet Urren. Pops at Qaebdol Cave Village and the Ancestor Stones themselves belong to this Faction. They are more concerned with keeping to their traditions and providing shelter to the plainsfolk from raids than in any conquest of their own.

### Travel Routes

* Taem's Oasis and the Salt Flats can only be accessed through the Dunes of Tor — for Asha Dunewalker members and any who gain their protection, either of these routes can be traveled in three ticks.
* The Ancestor Stones can be traveled to from Qaebdol Cave Village in a single tick for members of the Stonecallers or any who are granted their permission. (Otherwise, the Ancestor Stones can be reached normally.)
* Hiparun's Rift can only be accessed through the Ulum Highlands — for Hiparunites and any they deem worthy, this route can be traveled in two ticks.
* Any other combination of PopLocations can be traveled between freely, with no special rules applied.
