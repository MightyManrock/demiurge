# The Ledger and the Ash — Location & Civilization Ages

Ages for astronomical bodies are given in years. Ages for constructed artificial structures are given in years since construction. Civilization ages are given in years since first reaching Nascent scale. `None` means the location inherits its formation date from its parent.

---

## The Vireth Expanse
**Age:** ~4.8 billion years
*A younger, denser galaxy with active stellar formation and high stellar activity.*

### The Quelath System
**Age:** ~5.1 billion years
*A mature yellow dwarf — stable, middle-aged, hospitable to long civilizational development. Formation **preceded** the formation of its parent galaxy.*

- **Quelath I** — `None`
  - Quelath I Surface — `None`
  - The Deeps of Quelath I — `None` *(natural abyssal sub-location)*
- **Quelath II** — `None`
  - The Iron Deeps — **~400 years** *(constructed mining complex; established during the Hegemony's interplanetary expansion)*
- **The Quelath Belt** — `None`
  - The Spindle — **~800 years** *(hollowed asteroid settlement; the Belt Nomads' nearest thing to a capital, developed incrementally)*

**Civilizations:**

## The Mourne Reach
**Age:** ~11.2 billion years
*An older, more diffuse galaxy. Most stars are in late life stages. Morrha has historically felt more at home here.*
