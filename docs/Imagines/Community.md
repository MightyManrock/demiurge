# Community

**Thematic arc:** Domestic belonging and shared sustenance → Self-governance and collective memory → The civilization-spanning project or the people who carry themselves through displacement → The fundamental condition of togetherness.

## Tier 1

### The Hearth and Home

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The fire at the center belongs to everyone in the room. That is what makes it a hearth rather than just a fire.*

**Description:** Domestic belonging, the family unit, the shared warmth, the first and smallest community—the fire that is everyone's and therefore no one's to take.

**Mechanics:** `community:0.35, fire:0.1, decay:-0.1, sedentism:0.2, patience:0.15`

### The Shared Table

**Tooltip Blurb:** Eating *together is not incidental to community. It is community, stated plainly.*

**Description:** The communal meal, the ritual of shared food, is the most fundamental expression of collective belonging. What is given to the table is given to all.

**Mechanics:** `community:0.3, water:0.1, secrecy:-0.1, cooperation:0.15, humility:0.15`

## Tier 2

### The Village Council

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The decision that everyone has argued about is the decision everyone will live with.*

**Description:** Community as collective governance—the group deliberates and decides together. Belonging is expressed through shared responsibility.

**Mechanics:** `community:0.55, truth:0.15, conflict:-0.05, cooperation:0.15, honesty:0.2, patience:0.15`

### The Festival of Remembrance

**Tooltip Blurb:** *The celebration is not about what happened. It is about the fact that it happened to all of us.*

**Description**: Bound by shared history,  collective rituals keep common memory alive, reminding people they have a story together.

**Mechanics:** `community:0.5, light:0.12, change:-0.04, ancestor_worship:0.2, sincerity:0.15, humility:0.1, cooperation:0.1`

## Tier 3 — Left (requires The Village Council)

### The Unbroken Chain

**Tooltip Blurb:** *Each generation inherits a debt it did not agree to and passes on a gift it will not see received.*

**Description:** Civilization is the multigenerational project of building something that outlasts every individual within it; it is belonging as obligation across time.

**Mechanics:** `community:0.8, mastery:0.45, void:-0.02, cooperation:0.5, erudition:0.2, tenacity:0.2, idealism:0.1`

## Tier 3 — Right (requires The Festival of Remembrance)

### The Great Migration

**Tooltip Blurb:** *They carried everything that mattered. Some of it was portable.*

**Description:** The people carry their identity through displacement, remaining themselves across distances that should have dissolved them. Belonging is something you hold rather than somewhere you live.

**Mechanics:** `community:0.75, change:0.4, order:-0.02, nomadism:0.45, cooperation:0.2, tenacity:0.2, folk_wisdom:0.15`

## Tier 4 (requires all previous)

### The One Body

**Tooltip Blurb:** Community is the fundamental condition of belonging, the irreducible fact of togetherness that precedes every specific form it takes.

**Description:** The divine concept of Community itself.

**Mechanics:** `community:1.0`
